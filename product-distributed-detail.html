Distributed C&I Storage

Product Overview

Standardised outdoor cabinets integrating LFP cells, BMS, EMS, high-efficiency thermal management (air or liquid cooling), fire-safety systems, modular PCS and remote monitoring. Three configurations cover the C&I deployment range:

  • 51.2 V / 100 Ah — small-scale C&I
  • 51.2 V / 200 Ah — medium C&I
  • PCS 100 kW / 215 kWh · AC 380 V — flagship distributed unit
  • PCS 186 kW / 372 kWh · AC 690 V — large C&I and grid-area
