Source-Grid-Side Storage

Product Overview

The source-grid-side platform covers single-machine PCS units from 10 kW to 1,725 kW and integrated converter-transformer units to 3,450 kW. Two PCS platforms (1000 V and 1500 V) and an integrated string-type converter-transformer family address residential, C&I, and utility-scale storage stations.

1500 V String PCS (200 kW)

Modular cluster-level architecture eliminates inter-cluster mismatch and DC arc-flash risk; supports new/old battery mixing and online switching between grid-following and grid-forming (VSG) modes. Rated 186 / 215 kW with < 30 ms charge/discharge switching and IEC 61850 / EtherCAT carrier-level communication.

Integrated Converter-Transformer (1500 V)

PCS, step-up transformer (oil or dry), 35 kV switchgear, communication / auxiliary cabinets in a single integrated enclosure. Compartmentalised design — high-voltage room, transformer room and inverter room separated by double-steel-plate rock-wool insulation. Ratings to 4,464 / 5,160 kVA, 35 kV; IP66 control unit / IP54 other; −40 °C to +60 °C operating range; 4,000 m altitude.
