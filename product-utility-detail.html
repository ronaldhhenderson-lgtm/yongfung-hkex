Source-Grid-Side Storage源网侧储能源網側儲能

Product Overview产品概述產品概述

The source-grid-side platform covers single-machine PCS units from 10 kW to 1,725 kW and integrated converter-transformer units to 3,450 kW. Two PCS platforms (1000 V and 1500 V) and an integrated string-type converter-transformer family address residential, C&I, and utility-scale storage stations.源网侧平台覆盖10 kW至1,725 kW单机PCS及3,450 kW集成升压一体机。两大PCS平台（1000 V与1500 V）与组串式升压一体机产品族，覆盖户用、工商业及大型电站。源網側平台覆蓋10 kW至1,725 kW單機PCS及3,450 kW整合升壓一體機。兩大PCS平臺（1000 V與1500 V）與組串式升壓一體機產品族，覆蓋戶用、工商業及大型電站。
