Grid-Area Storage & Protocol Converters

Product Overview

Grid-area integrated storage and protocol-conversion platform serving the distribution layer of the grid. Three deployment configurations:

  • Grid-Area Storage 50 kW / 100 kWh · AC 380 V
  • Grid-Area Storage 100 kW / 215 kWh · AC 380 V
  • Grid-Supporting Converter 186 kW / 372 kWh · AC 690 V

Grid-Supporting Converter

Three-phase four-wire direct grid-connection with advanced DPWM / SVPWM modulation. Combines four functions into a single unit:

PCS + SVG + APF + Protocol Converter

  • Primary frequency regulation and inertia support (VSG)
  • Distribution-network three-phase imbalance treatment
  • Active and reactive power independent control
  • Distribution-grid dispatchable
  • Harmonic treatment and power-quality conditioning

PV / Storage Protocol Converter

Bridges the State Grid 698.45 command protocol with legacy inverter Modbus / 485 protocols, enabling the State Grid "PV Four Capabilities" soft-control mandate for distributed photovoltaic.

ParameterValue
Upstream communication4G to grid main station
Downstream communicationHPLC / RS-485
Upstream protocol698.45 (State Grid)
Downstream protocolModbus / 485
Compliance targetState Grid "PV Four Capabilities" — visibility, controllability, adjustability, dispatchability

Shared-Storage Architecture

Third-party shared-storage stations operate under a capacity-lease model — the storage operator builds the asset, leases capacity to demand-side parties, and the grid coordinates dispatch. Resolves three structural issues in the current new-energy paired-storage market:

  • Fragmented self-built storage assets with low utilisation rates
  • Absent revenue mechanisms for grid-side storage
  • Unified scheduling and settlement

Typical Deployment

Distribution-grid edge nodes, county-level rooftop PV aggregation, industrial-park grid-area storage, and shared-storage stations under capacity-lease commercial models.
