Ruggedised Portable Storage

Product Overview

The ruggedised portable storage line is built around four proprietary control boards — Inverter Control, MPPT Controller, BMS Battery Protection, and Main Control — paired with foldable PV panels and portable wind generation backpacks. Designed for industrial and infrastructure deployments where grid power is intermittent or unavailable.

Engineered for harsh-environment durability: validated against extreme temperature, dust ingress, salt-spray, vibration, and EMC standards. Multi-variant product family covers a wide range of operating conditions.

01

Inverter Control Board

Manages DC→AC conversion with high-efficiency switching topology and load-adaptive output regulation.

02

MPPT Controller

Maximum power point tracking for PV input — optimises harvest from the foldable solar panel under variable irradiance and shading.

03

BMS Battery Protection

Cell-level voltage / current / temperature telemetry, balancing, and over-charge / over-discharge protection.

04

Main Control Board

Coordinates inverter, MPPT and BMS subsystems; presents the SOC display and user interface; handles communications.

05

Foldable PV Panel

Lightweight, deployable solar input — provides off-grid recharge capability without infrastructure.

06

Wind Backpack

Portable wind-generation accessory for prolonged off-grid operation in environments with adequate wind resource.
