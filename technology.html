TECHNOLOGY ADVANTAGES

Proprietary core technologies across the storage value chain.

From cell management to grid-forming control, RongFung has developed a portfolio of differentiated technologies that deliver measurable advantages in safety, efficiency and deployment speed.

All-in-One Distributed Storage

Integration of LFP cells, BMS, EMS, high-efficiency liquid-cooling thermal management, fire-safety systems, modular PCS and remote monitoring into a single standardised outdoor cabinet.

  • ◆ Three-phase four-wire direct low-voltage grid connection, eliminating transformers
  • ◆ Advanced DPWM / SVPWM modulation for loss optimisation
  • ◆ Edge intelligence gateway — sub-10 µs control latency across 65,536 nodes
  • ◆ First in China to complete AI-based distributed-storage warning deployment
02

Real-Time EtherCAT Bus Control

Shortens system response time and improves frequency-regulation K-value. A single controller aggregates 16 PCS units through an EtherCAT real-time bus and consolidates battery, switchgear, transformer, fire-safety and auxiliary data upstream to the EMS.

03

Grid-Forming (VSG) Converter

County-level rooftop PV typically connects at weak points in rural feeder networks, producing high-impedance grid conditions. Configuring the converter as grid-forming with Virtual Synchronous Generator (VSG) technology fundamentally solves weak-grid stability.

  • Virtual synchronous machine control — frequency and voltage support
  • Dual-mode on-line switching between grid-following and grid-forming
  • Multi-unit off-grid parallel operation; single-unit IEC 61850 / EtherCAT carrier-level communication

PV / Storage Protocol Converter

The terminal communicates upstream over 4G and downstream over HPLC / RS-485, using the unified 698.45 protocol to address PV protocol converters. The converter parses 698.45 messages into Modbus commands understood by legacy inverters — and vice versa — enabling the State Grid "PV Four Capabilities" soft-control mandate for distributed solar.

String vs. Centralised — our architectural choice

String vs. Centralised — our architectural choice

RongFung favours the string architecture over traditional centralised solutions for 200 kW and below, delivering measurable improvements across four dimensions.

Dimension Centralised RongFung String
System efficiency Cluster imbalance reduces usable capacity Per-cluster management, old/new cell mixing supported
Safety DC arc-flash risk from HV parallel busing Zero DC arc-flash risk (no DC combining)
Reliability Single-PCS failure shuts down the entire system Redundant modular PCS — single-unit failure isolated
O&M convenience Centralised PCS — long replacement downtime Modular PCS — fast swap, whole-cabinet swap optional
Deployment economics Custom civil works, transformer rooms, long commissioning Pre-integrated outdoor cabinet — ≤ 7 days from delivery to grid-connection
Scalability Fixed-capacity architecture — difficult to expand Cabinet-level stacking, linear scale from 100 kWh to multi-MWh
SAFETY INTELLIGENCE

AI-Based Early-Warning & Fire-Safety Architecture

Multi-layer protection combining cell-level sensing, edge AI inference and active fire suppression — deployed in China's first AI-based distributed-storage warning system.

01

Cell-Level Sensing

Per-cell voltage, current and temperature telemetry at 1 Hz; internal impedance estimation for early degradation detection.

02

Edge AI Inference

On-gateway neural models score thermal-runaway precursors ahead of conventional threshold triggers, escalating alerts to EMS and fire-safety systems.

03

Active Suppression

Pack-level perfluorohexanone injection with directional venting and compartmentalised steel cabinet construction to isolate single-pack events.
