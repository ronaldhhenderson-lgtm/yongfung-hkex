VI / PV DEVICE LINE

PV Electronics — Micro-Inverters, RSD, Optimisers, Protocol Converters

A vertically-integrated PV electronics line addresses the "module-level optimisation + grid compliance" stack demanded by county-level distributed PV.

01

Micro-Inverter

Per-module MPPT, built-in rapid shutdown, IP67, 25-year warranty target; suitable for residential rooftop PV.

02

Rapid Shutdown Device

Module-level DC shutdown conforming to NEC 2017/2020 690.12 and equivalent PRC safety codes; triggered by PLC signal from inverter.

03

Power Optimiser

Mitigates mismatch losses from shading, soiling and ageing — recovers 8–25% energy yield versus conventional string configurations.

04

PV Protocol Converter

698.45 ⇄ Modbus bridge enabling State Grid "Four Capabilities" compliance for inverters deployed prior to the mandate.
