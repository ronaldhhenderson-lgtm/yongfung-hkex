Policy Landscape

Policy Landscape

Globally, storage is supported by nation-level strategies and industrial policies; in China, mandates shift from point to plane, with binding storage-pairing requirements at generation and grid levels.

Region Policy Instrument Key Content
China 14th Five-Year Storage Goals Nearly 30 provinces mandate storage pairing at 10–20% capacity with ≥2-hour duration (up to 4h in some provinces).
Guangdong High-Quality Storage Development Guidance Target storage-industry revenue of RMB 600 bn by 2025, RMB 1 trn by 2027.
United States Inflation Reduction Act From 2023, standalone storage qualifies for 30% investment tax credit over ten years.
European Union Clean Energy Package Regulation 2019/943 and Directive 2019/944 support residential storage growth and remove financial barriers.
Australia ARENA Grid-Scale Funding AUD 100 m grid-scale storage funding round announced in 2022.

Market Tailwinds

Market Tailwinds

Policy + Economics Dual Driver

User-side storage supported by local mandates in China and subsidies in Europe, US and Australia. Widening peak-valley price differentials improve commercial storage returns at 12–28% under 4:1 differentials.

Low Residential-Storage Penetration

Residential storage penetration in Germany, USA, Japan and Australia remains around 1% of detached homes — with PV penetration at c.10%, there is more than 10× headroom.

Shared-Storage Mechanisms

Third-party shared-storage stations under capacity-lease models unlock grid-scale participation and solve fragmented-asset utilisation.
