ABOUT THE COMPANY

A focused specialist in new-type energy storage.

Established in August 2022 in Shenzhen, YongFung Digital Energy is committed to the R&D, production and commercialisation of storage products.

Corporate Overview

YongFung Digital Energy was established in August 2022 to provide end-to-end energy-storage solutions.

In May 2024 the Group established YongFung Xi'an to address State Grid and China Southern Power Grid distribution pain-points.
