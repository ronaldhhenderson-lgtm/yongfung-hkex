ENERGY SOLUTIONS

Integrated "Source-Grid-Load-Storage" architecture.

In line with China's dual-carbon goals, we build clean, low-carbon, safe and high-efficiency energy systems at regional and industrial-park scale.

The New Power System

Building a new power system with renewables at its core is a central policy decision under China's dual-carbon goals, demanding coordinated adjustments in both the energy mix and the institutional framework of the power sector.

RongFung's integrated approach combines "green supply" with "green consumption", delivering solutions that serve both grid operators and industrial-park owners.

01

Regional-Scale Solution

Multi-site orchestration of utility-scale wind, solar and storage assets with distribution-level load management — deployed via our cloud platform for virtual power plant (VPP) aggregation and demand response.

02

Industrial-Park Solution

End-to-end integration of rooftop PV, storage, EV charging, distribution and on-site load management through a localised multi-energy control platform — enabling cloud-to-edge orchestration and a digital, low-carbon park micro-grid.

Shared Storage Stations

Shared Storage Stations

Independent third-party storage stations operated under a capacity-lease model. Shared storage solves two persistent issues: low utilisation of new-energy captive systems and the absence of market mechanisms for monetisation.
